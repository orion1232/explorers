Explorer Post 911

Explorer Post 911 is the premier experience and orientation program for young people considering a career in criminal justice. The mission of the Post is to offer young adults, ages 12-21, a behind the scenes look at the criminal justice field through training, practical exercises, and community activities.

  • Ride Along program for eligible Explorers at age 14
  • Week long end of year Explorer Academy
  • Annual National Competition in Gatlinburg, TN.
  • Uniform Provided
  • End of year trip to theme park

BELOW IS A LIST OF REQUIREMENTS TO JOIN

The following is a list of the minimum requirements to join and remain in the Explorer Program:

  • Live within St. Johns County.
  • Be at least 12 years of age and may stay until their 21st birthday.
  • Enrolled in a school: public, private, or home school, or have a high school diploma or G.E.D If a high school diploma or G.E.D is obtained, the member must be enrolled in continuing education (police academy, college/university, or trade school).
  • Candidates, Cadets and Explorers must maintain a 'C' (2.0) grade point average.
  • Chain of Command (Corporals, Sergeants, Lieutenants, and Captain) must maintain a 'B' (3.0) grade point average.
  • No criminal history.
  • Willing to participate in community service projects.
  • High moral standards and conduct within the community.
  • Pay an annual due of $120.00
